About us

SpaceX is a privat company that is setup to launch on contract from Nasa (National Aeronautics and Space Administration). SpaceX is a company that is owned by Elon Musk.

Our mission

The contract that spaceX has with NASA is a mission to bring cost of launching rockets into space down. This is done with the advent of rockets that is "reuseable" this can be see on each launch where strap-on bootster is landing on its own platforms.

The plan
